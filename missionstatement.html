ORGANIZATION'S INFORMATION

The core vision of Eleos Mission is to promote poor and deprived children education especially for the children in need of care and protection The primary aim of Eleos Mission is a social transformation and sustainable development of the poor people to lead a life with dignity and self-respect in a just and equitable society. It strives to initiate education and empowerment for integrated development that will expel poverty and help to create an enabling environment for holistic development. We work with the marginalized communities for the inclusive participation of their own problems.


VISION

“A just and equitable society with greater emphasis on spirituality, compassion, and peace on earth”, holistic community health care, where oppression, exploitation, and denial of life is confronted and transformed.”


MISSION

To witness Christ in word & deed. Establish native local disciples and leaders for mission and ministry. We strive to assist orphan, poor, neglected children, and their families as co-workers to promote mutually caring, loving, and humane communities. It believes in the sanctity of all life and preservation of all God’s creation. We work towards the empowerment of rural and urban slum dwellers through an integrated and holistic approach, which embodies awareness, education, economic, health, sanitation, capacity building with sustainable development.


GOAL

To initiate awareness and develop the remote villages spread education for holistic development, social Integral and community transformation of the tribal, Dalit and other weaker sections of the areas of Orissa, India. To form family-based prayer fellowship. To reach out to the unreached, untouched, and unserved areas to serve them with Gospel and holistic transformation of the communities. The particular objectives are organizing people to strive for social justice, to promote gender equality, to promote community involvement for sustainable development.
