Our Focus

_________________________________


To create a conducive and enabling environment for children of excluded and marginalized communities to facilitate their access to quality and relevant education in the project areas. To provide foster care and shelter homes and a safe environment for the children who are orphans. To facilitate school drop-out children, access to employment through skill development training, and credit linkages. To promote target groups’ access to rights and entitlements through information sharing, capacity building, and people-centric advocacy. The objectives will be achieved by using various means and ways of reaching out to these children. ACTIVITIES OF THE ORGANIZATION: PREACHING GOODNEWS AND ESTABLISHING NATIVE CHURCH PLANTING: To preach Good news to the unreached and unserved areas to transform the community holistically. To work for the holistic transformation of the individual and community. To participate in the individual & Community problems and involve the community to solve them collectively by the community participation. To train local native committed and dedicated youths to establish home-based prayer fellowships. Establishing home-based family prayer fellowships and village community prayer fellowships.


EDUCATION


To provide an opportunity of Preschool education to eligible children in the needy target Villages and to support already existing Government or to start Education centers in our target villages. To improve the education level of school going children by running Tuition Centres In our target villages. To empower and motivate village leadership to participate in the management of Education centers. To develop skills and train youths especially girls in self-reliance and self-employment activities in the target area villages. 5. To strive for the development of the number of community learning centers for the adult illiterate rural masses. To run and establish Orphanages and children’s homes. To conduct home-based family-based care homes for rural remote children who lack basic schooling facilities.


HEALTH AND NUTRITION


To improve prevalent malnutrition levels in preschool children and undernutrition Status of school going children through nutrition supplementation program in our target villages. To provide periodic healthcare services to children and women especially, besides in our target villages.


COMMUNITY HEALTH


To sensitize and empower the rural populace, especially youths about issues related to HIV/Aids and Planned Parenthood. To train and equip Women in safe pregnancy/delivery and neonatal care skills in our target villages. To teach primary health care and provide periodic health check-ups and treatment to vulnerable groups of children/women in our target villages.


COMMUNITY ORGANIZATION


Formation of Community Based Organization involving a various sections of the The rural community especially women and children and facilitate their participation, leadership development, entrepreneurship, leadership development and managerial skills.


LIVELIHOOD INTERVENTIONS


To make villagers aware, empower and motivate and facilitate agriculture based Livelihood resources. To promote organic farming. To develop and to strive the existing livelihood and food security program. To initiate ideas of self-realization, gender equality, and women empowerment. To promote and help to generate employment and livelihood promotion. To develop sustainable livelihood concerns and initiations Providing foster care and support to children in the Foster Home at Rayagada district of Odisha. Initiate community learning centers in the rural remote villages to create a child-friendly environment in education and participation for children.
