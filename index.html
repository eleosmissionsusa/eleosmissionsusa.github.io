Mission Statement

o witness Christ in word & deed. Establish native local disciples and leaders for mission and ministry. We strive to assist the orphan, poor, neglected children and their families as co-workers to promote mutually caring, loving and humane communities. We believes in the sanctity of all life and preservation of all God’s creation. We work towards empowerment of rural and urban slum dwellers through integrated and holistic approach, which embodies awareness, education, economic, health, sanitation, capacity building with sustainable development.
